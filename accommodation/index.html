In every room

A kitchenette and ensuite of your own

Each room has its own kitchenette — fridge/freezer, microwave, and kettle — so you can keep things simple and self-contained. Add free WiFi, a smart TV, and reverse-cycle air conditioning, and you have a comfortable base for work or leisure.

Frequently asked questions

How do I book a room?

Bookings are taken directly by phone on 0431 295 126 or by email at info@royalgympie.com.au. Our on-site managers will confirm availability and rates.

What are the rates?

$130 per night for a king single, $150 per night for a double or queen, and a flat $600 for a 7-night weekly stay (serviced once a week). Discounted rates apply for weekly stays.

What is included in each room?

Air conditioning, a kitchenette with fridge/freezer, microwave, and kettle, an ensuite bathroom, free WiFi, and a smart TV. Continental breakfast is available.

Can I stay for work or a longer period?

Yes. The rooms suit overnight, multi-night, and weekly stays. Weekly guests enjoy a flat rate and a weekly room service.

Where is the hotel located?

188–190 Mary Street, in central Gympie, Queensland — a short walk to shops, cafes, restaurants, art galleries, the bus station, and the Queensland Rail connection bus. See the contact page for directions.
