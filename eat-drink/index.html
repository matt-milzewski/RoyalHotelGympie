Functions & Facilities

A heritage venue for private functions, plus comfortable shared facilities for our accommodation guests.

Private functions

The bar and entertainment areas, all to yourselves

The Royal's bar and entertainment areas are no longer open to the public daily, but they are available for private bookings by appointment. With more than 140 years of character and a central Gympie address, they make a memorable setting for functions, conferences, and special gatherings.

Tell us what you have in mind and we'll talk through the space, timing, and what works best for your group.
